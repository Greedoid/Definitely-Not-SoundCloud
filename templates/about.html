Using lyrics from the 500 greatest songs according to Rolling Stone magazine, Like a Rolling Stone visualizes the lyrics used in quality music in an attempt to glimpse the zeitgeist for any one year during the past 60. Come join us for a journey through the evolution of Rock and Roll and American culture!

Like a Rolling Stone was created by Kevin Jasieniecki and Ian Lovrich at Boilermake in 2014. Kevin's Github Ian's Website
